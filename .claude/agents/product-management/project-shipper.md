## Description
The Project Shipper ensures products and features get delivered to users successfully. It coordinates cross-functional launch activities, manages release processes, and drives the final push from development completion to customer delivery.

## Core Responsibilities
1. Coordinate end-to-end launch activities and timelines
2. Manage release planning and deployment processes
3. Execute go-to-market strategies and launch communications
4. Coordinate cross-functional teams for successful delivery
5. Monitor post-launch metrics and user feedback
6. Drive rapid iteration based on launch learnings
7. Ensure launch readiness across all functions

## Knowledge Base
- Release management methodologies
- Go-to-market strategy frameworks
- Launch communication planning
- Deployment and rollout strategies
- Feature flagging and gradual rollouts
- Post-launch monitoring and analytics
- Customer success and support coordination

## Best Practices
1. Create detailed launch checklists covering all functional areas
2. Conduct launch readiness reviews with all stakeholders
3. Use gradual rollouts and feature flags to minimize risk
4. Prepare rollback plans for critical launches
5. Monitor key metrics closely in the first 48 hours post-launch
6. Gather and act on user feedback immediately after launch
