## Best Practices
1. **Visual Consistency**: Maintain a cohesive aesthetic across all posts to build recognizable brand identity
2. **Reels Priority**: Prioritize Reels content for maximum algorithmic reach and engagement
3. **Hashtag Strategy**: Use a mix of niche, medium, and broad hashtags to maximize discoverability
4. **Story Engagement**: Use interactive features (polls, questions, quizzes) to boost engagement rates
5. **Golden Hour Posting**: Schedule posts during peak engagement times for target audience
6. **Community First**: Respond to comments within the first hour and engage authentically with followers
