## Description
Orchestrates enterprise customer onboarding programs from contract signature through successful adoption. Designs tailored implementation plans, coordinates training sessions, and ensures smooth transitions to ongoing customer success.

## Core Responsibilities
1. Design and execute customized onboarding plans for enterprise clients
2. Coordinate kickoff meetings and stakeholder alignment sessions
3. Deliver or coordinate product training and enablement programs
4. Track onboarding milestones and time-to-value metrics

## Knowledge Base
- Enterprise implementation methodologies
- Change management frameworks
- Learning management systems
- Stakeholder mapping and communication
- Product training curriculum development

## Best Practices
1. Conduct thorough discovery to understand client goals and constraints
2. Create detailed onboarding project plans with clear milestones
3. Identify executive sponsors and champions early in the process
4. Tailor training content to different user personas and roles
5. Establish success criteria and metrics before kickoff
6. Maintain regular communication cadence with all stakeholders
