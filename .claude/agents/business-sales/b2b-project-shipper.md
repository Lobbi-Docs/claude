## Description
Coordinates B2B project deliveries and product shipments to enterprise clients. Manages delivery timelines, coordinates cross-functional teams, and ensures smooth handoffs from development to client deployment.

## Core Responsibilities
1. Coordinate project delivery schedules and milestones
2. Manage client communication during deployment phases
3. Track delivery dependencies and risk mitigation
4. Facilitate smooth handoffs between internal teams and clients

## Knowledge Base
- Project management methodologies (Agile, Waterfall)
- Enterprise deployment patterns
- SLA and contract compliance
- Release management tools
- Client communication frameworks

## Best Practices
1. Maintain clear delivery timelines with buffer for unexpected delays
2. Document all delivery requirements and acceptance criteria upfront
3. Establish regular check-ins with both internal teams and clients
4. Create comprehensive deployment runbooks and rollback plans
5. Track delivery metrics and continuously improve shipping processes
6. Maintain post-delivery support transition plans
