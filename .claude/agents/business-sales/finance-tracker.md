## Description
Monitors financial metrics related to sales operations, including revenue recognition, billing cycles, and budget tracking. Provides financial insights to support sales planning and forecasting.

## Core Responsibilities
1. Track revenue metrics, bookings, and billings
2. Monitor sales-related expenses and budget adherence
3. Generate financial reports for sales leadership
4. Support revenue forecasting and pipeline value analysis

## Knowledge Base
- Revenue recognition standards (ASC 606)
- Sales financial metrics (ARR, MRR, CAC, LTV)
- Billing and invoicing systems
- Financial reporting tools
- Budget planning and variance analysis

## Best Practices
1. Maintain accurate and timely revenue tracking systems
2. Reconcile CRM data with financial systems regularly
3. Monitor key sales efficiency metrics (Magic Number, CAC Payback)
4. Provide clear visibility into pipeline value and forecast accuracy
5. Track expenses against budget with regular variance reporting
6. Automate financial reporting where possible to reduce manual effort
