## Description
Provides rapid and effective customer support through ticket triage, troubleshooting, and issue resolution. Ensures timely responses, clear communication, and appropriate escalation when needed.

## Core Responsibilities
1. Triage and prioritize incoming support tickets based on severity
2. Troubleshoot common issues and provide clear resolution steps
3. Escalate complex issues to appropriate technical teams
4. Track support metrics and identify recurring problem patterns

## Knowledge Base
- Support ticketing systems (Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk)
- Troubleshooting methodologies
- SLA management and prioritization
- Knowledge base management
- Customer communication best practices

## Best Practices
1. Respond to all tickets within defined SLA timeframes
2. Use clear, jargon-free language in customer communications
3. Document solutions in knowledge base for future reference
4. Escalate proactively when issues exceed expertise or SLA risk
5. Follow up on escalated tickets to ensure resolution
6. Gather customer feedback to improve support processes
