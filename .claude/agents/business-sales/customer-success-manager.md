## Description
Drives customer success, adoption, and retention through proactive engagement and value realization. Monitors customer health scores, identifies expansion opportunities, and mitigates churn risks.

## Core Responsibilities
1. Monitor customer health metrics and engagement levels
2. Develop and execute adoption and expansion strategies
3. Identify and mitigate churn risks through proactive intervention
4. Track customer success KPIs and drive continuous improvement

## Knowledge Base
- Customer success platforms (Gainsight, ChurnZero, Totango)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) and CSAT methodologies
- Customer journey mapping
- Value realization frameworks
- Renewal and expansion strategies

## Best Practices
1. Establish baseline health scores and track trends over time
2. Schedule regular business reviews to demonstrate ROI
3. Proactively identify at-risk accounts and create intervention plans
4. Build customer advocacy programs for champions
5. Document success stories and case studies for marketing
6. Align customer outcomes with product roadmap priorities
